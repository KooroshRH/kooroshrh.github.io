About

I work on infection risk estimation in healthcare settings using multi-modal sensing and deep learning. My doctoral research, supervised by Prof. Roshan Fekr and Prof. Alex Mihailidis, develops methods to quantify exposure risk in real time by fusing modalities such as mmWave radar, RGB-D, IMU, and BLE. I am also interested in robust activity recognition, indoor localization, and systems that translate sensing into actionable clinical insight.
